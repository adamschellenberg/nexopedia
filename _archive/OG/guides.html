Effect Description
Bind The affected Nexomon can't be swapped for another one. Receives damage each turn equal to 8% of its maximum HP.
Burn The affected Nexomon receives damage each turn equal to 8% of its maximum HP.
Confusion The affected Nexomon has a 35% chance of failing when using a skill. If this happens, it receives damage equal to 8% of its maximum HP.
Cursed All skills that the affected Nexomon uses are "not effective"
Demotivation The affected Nexomon has a 50% chance of failing when using a skill.
Frail The affected Nexomon receives 30% extra damage from non-elemental attacks.
Freeze The affected Nexomon can't use any skills, unless it's a Fire skill (which also removes Freeze). Each turn there is a 30% chance that the Nexomon will thaw.
Hex The affected Nexomon receives damage each turn equal to 12% of its maximum HP.
Inception The affected Nexomon receives damage each turn equal to 15% of its maximum HP, if it's asleep. This effect is cured if the Nexomon is no longer asleep.
Invincible The affected Nexomon receives no damage from incoming attacks.
Leech The affected Nexomon receives damage each turn equal to 10% of its maximum HP. The opponent will be healed for the same amount, even if it's not the original cause of this status effect.
Nervous The affected Nexomon has a 60% chance of failing when using a skill, if it was using the same skill last turn.
Paralyze The affected Nexomon has a 30% chance of failing when using a skill.
Planted The affected Nexomon can neither be swapped or escape from the battle.
Poison The affected Nexomon receives damage each turn equal to 10% of its maximum HP.
Provoked The affected Nexomon can't inflict status effects.
Puzzled The affected Nexomon can only use the same skill as last turn, otherwise it loses its turn.
Recover The affected Nexomon restores HP each turn equal to 8% of its maximum HP.
Sick The affected Nexomon can't be healed. Incoming heals are nullified.
Sleep The afftected Nexomon can't use any skills or evade attacks.
Survive The affected Nexomon won't drop below 1HP for the duration of this effect.

  • What is a cosmic Nexomon? Cosmic Nexomon are the equivalent to shiny Pokemon. Color variants of the Nexomon you know and love.
  • How will I know if a Nexomon is cosmic? Cosmic Nexomon name boxes will flash red, like in the gif below.
  • What are the odds of finding a cosmic Nexomon? The rate of finding a cosmic Nexomon starts at 1/4000 (0.025%) at the beginning of the game. However, as you progress through the game, this oods will increase as follows:
    • Odds increase to 1/3500 (0.029%) after saving Ignitia, and increases again to 1/3000 (0.033%) after completing the Drake Isles chapter
    • The rate increases by 1 point for each uniquely seen Nexomon. Example: 1/3000 odds are increased to 1/2950, if you have seen 50 different Nexomon.
    • The rate increases by 2 points for each uniquely owned Nexomon. Example: 1/2950 odds are increased to 1/2900, if you have captured 50 different Nexomon.
    • The rate increases 50 points for each Cosmic Charm owned. Example: 1/2900 odds are increased to 1/2800, if two Cosmic Charms are owned.
    • The best possible odds of finding a cosmic Nexomon are 1/1607 (0.0622%), if you are in the late-game, have captured every Nexomon, and have found all five Cosmic Charms.
