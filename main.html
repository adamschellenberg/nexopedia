Welcome to Nexopedia!
Nexopedia is a community-supported Nexomon encyclopedia. Because this website is fully developed and updated by fans, things are always changing and being updated. If there is incorrect or missing information, please message us, or contribute to our database. All help and feedback is appreciated! Check back often to see all the new updates!
News and Updates
It's Alive and Current Updates Focus!
May 2021

Greetings, Tamers!

We're now live! Cheers! Being new, things are always changing around here. Because this is a passion project developed and updated during free time, some information may be incorrect or missing. If you notice anything missing, and you have the information, or if you notice something incorrect, and you know what it should be, please reach out at nexopedia@gmail.com!
Here are some things we are looking for especially:
  • Missing item icons (as pngs) on the Items page
  • Missing Nexomon sprites (normal and cosmic) on the Database page
  • A Nexopedia official logo

Thank you in advance for any contributions! Any help you give makes this site a better resource for everyone!

We are currently working on pretty-ing things up a bit around here. We hope to have a more responsive site soon to allow for a more mobile-friendly experience, for all you tamers-on-the-go! Until the next update, we hope we can be a useful resource to you as you enjoy the world of Nexomon!


Happy cosmic hunting!
