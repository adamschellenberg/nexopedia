Items

Medicine items are used in and out of battle to restore health or stamina, revive, or cure a status effect of a Nexomon. There are 22 medicine items.

  • Potion: Restores 40 HP.

  • Super Potion: Restores 80 HP.

  • Ultra Potion: Restores 120 HP.

  • Max Potion: Restores 200 HP.
  • Ether: Restores 60 stamina.

  • Super Ether: Restores 100 stamina.

  • Ultra Ether: Restores 150 stamina.

  • Max Ether: Restores 200 stamina.
  • Elixir: Restores both 50 HP and 50 stamina.

  • Super Elixir: Restores both 90 HP and 90 stamina.

  • Ultra Elixir: Restores both 140 HP and 140 stamina.

  • Max Elixir: Restores both 200 HP and 200 stamina.
  • Revive: Restores a fainted Nexomon along with 50 HP.

  • Super Revive: Restores a fainted Nexomon along with 80 HP.

  • Ultra Revive: Restores a fainted Nexomon along with 120 HP.
  • Anti Burn: Cures the "BURNING" status effect on a Nexomon!

  • Anti Paralyze: Cures the "PARALYZED" status effect on a Nexomon!

  • Anti Poison: Cures the "POISONED" status effect on a Nexomon!

  • Anti Freeze: Cures the "FROZEN" status effect on a Nexomon!

  • Anti Sleep: Cures the "ASLEEP" status effect on a Nexomon!

  • Anti Confusion: Cures the "CONFUSED" status effect on a Nexomon!

  • Anti Bind: Cures the "BOUND" status effect on a Nexomon!

Cores can be equipped to Nexomon to increase stats or provide other benefits, up to 4 per Nexomon.

  • Destruction Core (I): Boosts the ATK stat by 4%.

  • Destruction Core (II): Boosts the ATK stat by 7%.

  • Destruction Core (III): Boosts the ATK stat by 10%.
  • Protection Core (I): Boosts the DEF stat by 4%.

  • Protection Core (II): Boosts the DEF stat by 7%.

  • Protection Core (III): Boosts the DEF stat by 10%.
  • Vitality Core (I): Boosts the HP stat by 5%.

  • Vitality Core (II): Boosts the HP stat by 7%.

  • Vitality Core (III): Boosts the HP stat by 12%.
  • Stamina Core (I): Boosts the STA stat by 6%.

  • Stamina Core (II): Boosts the STA stat by 12%.

  • Stamina Core (III): Boosts the STA stat by 15%.
  • Immunity Core (I): Decreases the chance of receiving status effects by 5%.

  • Immunity Core (II): Decreases the chance of receiving status effects by 10%.

  • Immunity Core (III): Decreases the chance of receiving status effects by 15%.
  • Experience Core (I): Boosts the EXP earned by 7%.

  • Experience Core (II): Boosts the EXP earned by 13%.

  • Experience Core (III): Boosts the EXP earned by 20%.
  • Synergy Core (I): Copies 8% of the EXP received by other team members without having to fight.

  • Synergy Core (II): Copies 16% of the EXP received by other team members without having to fight.

  • Synergy Core (III): Copies 25% of the EXP received by other team members without having to fight.
  • Greed Core (I): Boosts the amount of coins earned in battle by 3%.

  • Greed Core (II): Boosts the amount of coins earned in battle by 6%.

  • Greed Core (III): Boosts the amount of coins earned in battle by 9%.

Give to wild Nexomon to increase chances of catching. Different Nexomon prefer different foods!

  • Perfect Berry: The ultimate meal! There is no Nexomon that can resist this!

  • Strawberry Cake: A delicious cake with strawberry top.

  • Canele: An original recipe! Chocolate cake with a red berry gelatin top.

  • Lemon Pie: A rich lemon-flavored cake filled with delicious cream.

  • Onigiri: A traditional rice ball with subtle salty flavors.

  • Shrimpgiri: Delicious and fresh rice with a funny looking top. Ideal for youngsters.

  • Taco: Made of fresh ingredients and wrapped by a crunchy tortilla.

  • Lacreme: Vanilla flavored ice cream filled with fresh nuts and sweet cream.

  • Omelette: Only fresh eggs are used to create this masterpiece. Ideal for breakfast.

  • Pandulce: A simple dish, very simple to bake thus making it ideal for travelers.

  • Baguette: Especially made to remain fresh for days! It can be served with both sweet and salty dishes.

  • Donut: A classic among tamers. It is not recommended to eat in large portions.

  • Chocolate Muffin: A soft muffin filled with the sweetest chocolate you can find!

  • Flan: A wiggly dessert with a cherry on top. It is not too sweet, just enough to give it proper flavor.

  • Green Cake: A delicious cake made with the finest green tea.

  • Chocolate Cake: Only for chocolate fanatics. This rich flavored chocolate cake can be sometimes too sweet for some.

  • Glazed Cake: A delicious pie mixed with fresh berries and fruits on top.

  • Cream Puff: A creamy dessert, it melts almost immediately inside your mouth.

  • Pink Pecana: A colorful dish. Very popular among young girls and some Nexomon too!

  • Watermelon: A juicy fruit perfect for hot days. A favorite among tamers in the southern areas.

  • Orange: A sweet and juicy orange. It needs lots of sunshine and water to grow!

  • Banana: A standard banana. Hard to preserve for long periods.

  • Apple: A sweet apple, ideal for long trips. It is easily found in the outskirts of any town.

  • Grape: Usually used as an ingredient for desserts but it can also be eaten by itself. Very healthy!

  • Peach: A seasonal fruit. Grab them while you can!

  • Green Pear: It is said this fruit is not only delicious and easy to find but it is also used to make refreshing and healthy beverages.

  • Fries: Fast food. Good for a quick snack, very popular among young tamers.

  • Sausage: A product usually made of fake meat. Not the healthiest dish but definitely has a strong flavor.

  • Pincho: A simple snack that includes a variety of fresh and delicious ingredients.

  • Egg Tart: A very exclusive dish and it is not easy to cook. It requires a certain mastery to be cooked correctly.

  • Meat: A simple dish that packs a strong flavor and a great source of energy!
